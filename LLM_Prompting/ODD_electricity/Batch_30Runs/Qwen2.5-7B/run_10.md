### Action Situation 1: Capacitor Adoption Assurance Game (AS1)
**Title:** Capacitor Assurance Game
**Tension:** Coordination vs. Free-Rider Dilemma

**Matrix:**

|   | Farmer 2: Adopt | Farmer 2: Not Adopt |
|---|----------------|--------------------|
| **Farmer 1: Adopt** | (1, 1) | (0, 2) |
| **Farmer 1: Not Adopt** | (2, 0) | (0, 0) |

**Justification:**
- **Farmer 1 and Farmer 2:** Both farmers share the same transformer. If both adopt capacitors, voltage stability improves, benefiting both. However, if only one adopts, the improvement is minimal and may be difficult to attribute to the capacitor. Therefore, a unilateral adoption is less attractive, leading to a free-rider dilemma.

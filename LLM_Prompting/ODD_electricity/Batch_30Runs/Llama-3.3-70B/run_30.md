### Action Situation 1: Capacitor Adoption Coordination
#### Title: Mutual Investment in Voltage-Stabilizing Equipment
#### Tension: Farmers must decide whether to invest in capacitors, with mutual investment yielding shared improvement but unilateral investment yielding no added private benefit.
#### Matrix/Sequential Representation:
|  | Farmer 2 Invests | Farmer 2 Doesn't Invest |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Farmer 1 Invests** | 3, 3 | 1, 2 |
| **Farmer 1 Doesn't Invest** | 2, 1 | 2, 2 |
#### Justification: This action situation reflects the coordination problem between neighboring farmers who must decide whether to invest in voltage-stabilizing equipment. The payoff matrix shows that mutual investment (3, 3) is the best outcome, but if one farmer invests and the other doesn't, the investing farmer gets a lower payoff (1, 2).

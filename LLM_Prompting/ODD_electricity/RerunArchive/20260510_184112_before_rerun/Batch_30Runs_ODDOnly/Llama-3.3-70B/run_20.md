### Action Situation 1: Capacitor Adoption Assurance Game
#### Title: Farmer-Farmer Coordination
#### Tension: Mutual cooperation is needed for efficient voltage-stabilising equipment adoption.
#### Matrix/Sequential Representation:
|  | Farmer 2 Invests | Farmer 2 Does Not Invest |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Farmer 1 Invests** | 3, 3 | 1, 2 |
| **Farmer 1 Does Not Invest** | 2, 1 | 2, 2 |
#### Justification: This action situation reflects the coordination problem between two neighbouring farmers deciding whether to invest in voltage-stabilising equipment. Mutual investment yields shared improvement, while unilateral investment yields no added private benefit.

### Action Situation 2: Sequential Social Learning in Capacitor Adoption
#### Title: Farmer Social Learning
#### Tension: Imitation of successful peers in adopting capacitor measures.
#### Matrix/Sequential Representation:
- Farmer 1 adopts capacitor measure.
- Farmer 2 observes Farmer 1's outcome.
  - If outcome is successful, Farmer 2 adopts.
  - If outcome is not successful, Farmer 2 does not adopt.
#### Justification: This sequential action situation represents how farmers learn from each other's experiences with capacitor adoption, imitating successful outcomes.

### Action Situation 6: Groundwater-Extraction Prisoner’s Dilemma
#### Title: Farmer-Farmer Groundwater Extraction
#### Tension: Conflict between short-term gain from over-extraction and long-term sustainability of groundwater.
#### Matrix/Sequential Representation:
|  | Farmer 2 Restrains | Farmer 2 Over-Extracts |
| --- | --- | --- |

### Action Situation 2: Sequential Social-Learning Process in Capacitor Adoption
#### Title: Imitation of Successful Peers
#### Tension: Social Learning Dilemma
#### Matrix/Sequential Representation:
1. Farmer 1 adopts capacitor and observes outcome.
2. Farmer 2 observes Farmer 1's outcome and decides whether to adopt.
   - If Farmer 1's outcome is successful, Farmer 2 adopts.
   - If Farmer 1's outcome is unsuccessful, Farmer 2 does not adopt.
#### Justification: This sequential action situation represents the social-learning process where farmers observe and imitate the successful outcomes of their peers in adopting capacitors.

### Action Situation 2: Sequential Social Learning in Capacitor Adoption
#### Title: Farmer Social Learning
#### Tension: Imitation of successful peers versus misinterpretation of effects on performance.
#### Matrix/Sequential Representation:
1. Farmer 1 adopts capacitor and observes outcome.
2. Farmer 2 observes Farmer 1's outcome and decides to adopt or not based on its ranking.
#### Justification: This sequential action situation represents the social-learning process where each farmer observes a peer's outcome and imitates only if that outcome ranks higher.

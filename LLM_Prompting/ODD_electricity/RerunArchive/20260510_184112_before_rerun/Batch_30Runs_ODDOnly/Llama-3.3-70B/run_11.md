### Action Situation 4: Authorization-and-Investment Asymmetric Coordination Game
#### Title: Formal Cooperation vs. Opportunism
#### Tension: Farmer and staff have asymmetric incentives between formal cooperation and opportunistic behavior.
#### Sequential Representation:
1. Farmer (Formal/Informal Request)
2. Staff (Invest/Withhold Capacity)
- If Farmer chooses Formal and Staff chooses Invest: Farmer gains 3, Staff gains 2
- If Farmer chooses Formal and Staff chooses Withhold: Farmer loses 1, Staff gains 1
- If Farmer chooses Informal and Staff chooses Invest: Farmer gains 4, Staff loses 1
- If Farmer chooses Informal and Staff chooses Withhold: No change
#### Justification: This situation reflects the strategic tension between farmers and staff in choosing between formal requests and informal exchanges for capacity investment.

### Action Situation 6: Sequential Social-Learning Process in Capacitor Adoption
#### Title: Imitation and Diffusion of Capacitor Adoption
#### Tension: Farmers observe and imitate successful peers in adopting capacitor measures.
#### Sequential Representation:
1. Farmer 1 Adopt/Not Adopt
2. Farmer 2 Observes Outcome
3. Farmer 2 Imitates/Does Not Imitate
- If Farmer 1 Adopt and outcome is successful: Farmer 2 Imitates with probability p
- If Farmer 1 Adopt and outcome is not successful: Farmer 2 Does Not Imitate
- If Farmer 1 Not Adopt: No change
#### Justification: This situation models the sequential process of social learning where farmers observe the outcomes of their peers' decisions on capacitor adoption and make their own decisions based on that information.

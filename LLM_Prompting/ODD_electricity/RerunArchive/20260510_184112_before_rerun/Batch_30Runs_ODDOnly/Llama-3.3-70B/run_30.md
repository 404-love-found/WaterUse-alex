### Action Situation 1: Capacitor-Adoption Assurance Game
#### Title: Farmer-Farmer Coordination for Capacitor Adoption
#### Tension: Mutual cooperation is Pareto-dominant but risky.
#### Matrix/Sequential Representation:
|  | Farmer 2 Adopts | Farmer 2 Does Not Adopt |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Farmer 1 Adopts** | 3, 3 | 1, 2 |
| **Farmer 1 Does Not Adopt** | 2, 1 | 2, 2 |
#### Justification: This action situation reflects the strategic tension between two neighbouring farmers deciding whether to invest in voltage-stabilising equipment (capacitors). Mutual investment yields shared improvement, while unilateral investment yields no added private benefit.

### Action Situation 2: Sequential Social-Learning Process in Capacitor Adoption
#### Title: Farmer Social Learning for Capacitor Adoption
#### Tension: Imitation of successful peers leads to diffusion of capacitor adoption.
#### Matrix/Sequential Representation:
1. Farmer 1 adopts capacitor and observes outcome.
2. Farmer 2 observes Farmer 1's outcome and decides to adopt if outcome is better.
#### Justification: This action situation represents the sequential process of social learning among farmers, where each farmer observes a peer's outcome and imitates only if that outcome ranks higher.

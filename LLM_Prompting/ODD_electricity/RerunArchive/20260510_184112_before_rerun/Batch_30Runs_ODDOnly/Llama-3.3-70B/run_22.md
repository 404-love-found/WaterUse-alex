### Action Situation 1: Capacitor-Adoption Assurance Game
#### Title: Farmer-Farmer Coordination
#### Tension: Mutual investment in voltage-stabilising equipment yields shared improvement, while unilateral investment yields no added private benefit.
#### Matrix/Sequential Representation:
|  | Farmer 2 Invests | Farmer 2 Does Not Invest |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Farmer 1 Invests** | 3, 3 | 1, 2 |

#### Justification: This action situation reflects the strategic tension between two neighbouring farmers deciding whether to invest in capacitor adoption, with mutual cooperation being Pareto-dominant but risky.

### Action Situation 2: Sequential Social-Learning Process
#### Title: Farmer Social Learning
#### Tension: Each farmer observes a peer's outcome and imitates only if that outcome ranks higher.
#### Matrix/Sequential Representation:
- Farmer 1 adopts capacitor, outcome = Success/Failure
- Farmer 2 observes Farmer 1's outcome
  - If Success, Farmer 2 adopts capacitor
  - If Failure, Farmer 2 does not adopt capacitor
#### Justification: This sequential action situation represents the social-learning process among farmers, where each farmer decides whether to adopt a capacitor based on the observed outcome of another farmer.

### Action Situation 1: Capacitor Adoption Assurance Game (AS1)
**Title:** Farmer-Farmer Coordination on Capacitor Investment

**Tension:** Mutual investment in capacitor measures is beneficial, but unilateral investment yields no added private benefit.

**Matrix Representation:**

|          | Farmer 2 Invests | Farmer 2 Does Not Invest |
|----------|------------------|-------------------------|
| **Farmer 1 Invests** | (2, 2) | (1, 0) |
| **Farmer 1 Does Not Invest** | (0, 1) | (0, 0) |

**Justification:** This game captures the coordination problem where mutual investment in capacitor measures benefits both farmers by stabilizing voltage, but a single farmer's investment does not yield a private benefit, leading to a free-rider dilemma.

### Action Situation 2: Sequential Social-Learning Process (AS2)
**Title:** Farmer Social Learning on Capacitor Adoption

**Sequential Representation (Game Tree):**

```
               Farmer 1
                  |
           (C1) ----> Farmer 2
           /      \
         (C2)    (C3)
          /        \
       (C4)      (C5)
```

**Justification:** This game represents the sequential process where farmer 1 adopts a capacitor and farmer 2 observes and imitates if the outcome ranks higher. It captures the social learning mechanism where diffusion occurs only after a successful trial.

### Action Situation 3: Asymmetric Transformer-Capacity Authorization Dilemma (AS3)
**Title:** Farmer-Farmer Coordination on Transformer Capacity

**Matrix Representation:**

|          | Farmer 2 Authorizes | Farmer 2 Does Not Authorize |
|----------|---------------------|-----------------------------|
| **Farmer 1 Authorizes** | (1, 1) | (2, 0) |
| **Farmer 1 Does Not Authorize** | (0, 2) | (0, 0) |

**Justification:** This game captures the asymmetric dilemma where mutual authorization benefits both by raising voltage quality, but costs fall solely on the authorizer, creating a free-rider incentive.

### Action Situation 5: Asymmetric Authorization and Investment Game (AS5)
**Title:** Farmer-Sub-Station Staff Formal vs. Informal Request

**Matrix Representation:**

|          | Staff Invests | Staff Withholds Investment |

### Action Situation 6: Groundwater Extraction Prisoner's Dilemma (AS6)
**Title:** Farmer-Farmer Groundwater Extraction

**Matrix Representation:**

|          | Farmer 2 Extracts | Farmer 2 Does Not Extract |
|----------|-------------------|--------------------------|
| **Farmer 1 Extracts** | (2, 2) | (1, 3) |

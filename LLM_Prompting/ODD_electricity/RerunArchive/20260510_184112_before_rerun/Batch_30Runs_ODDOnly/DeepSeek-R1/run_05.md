**Action Situation 2: Sequential Social Learning in Capacitor Adoption**  
Tension: Farmers imitate peers only after observing successful coordinated adoption outcomes.

1. Early adopter Farmer A adopts capacitor → observes outcome  
2. Neighbor Farmer B observes A's outcome:

### Action Situation 5: Authorization and Enforcement Game
#### Title: Formal Access
#### Tension: Compliance vs. Evasion
#### Sequential Representation:
1. Farmer requests formal access.
2. Staff decides to enforce or tolerate.
   - If enforce, farmer pays fee, staff bears effort cost.
   - If tolerate, farmer saves fee, staff saves effort but risks oversight.
#### Justification: This sequential game represents the interaction between a farmer seeking formal access and staff deciding whether to enforce rules or tolerate informal access, considering costs, effort, and oversight risk.

### Action Situation 6: Social Learning and Capacitor Adoption
#### Title: Social Learning
#### Tension: Adoption vs. Non-Adoption
#### Sequential Representation:
1. Farmer observes neighbor's adoption outcome.
2. Farmer decides to adopt or not based on observed success.
#### Justification: This action situation captures the process of social learning where a farmer's decision to adopt a capacitor is influenced by observing the outcomes of neighboring farmers who have already adopted.

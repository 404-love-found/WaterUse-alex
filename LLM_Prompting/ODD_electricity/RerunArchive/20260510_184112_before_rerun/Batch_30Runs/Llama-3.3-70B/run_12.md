### Action Situation 1: Capacitor Adoption Assurance
#### Title: Coordinated Capacitor Adoption
#### Tension: Farmers must decide whether to invest in capacitors, with mutual benefit only if both adopt.
#### Matrix/Sequential Representation:
|  | Farmer 2 Adopt | Farmer 2 Not Adopt |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Farmer 1 Adopt** | 3, 3 | 1, 2 |
| **Farmer 1 Not Adopt** | 2, 1 | 2, 2 |
#### Justification: This action situation reflects the strategic tension between farmers deciding to adopt capacitors. Mutual adoption leads to improved voltage stability and higher payoffs, while unilateral adoption yields lower payoffs due to weak attribution of benefits.

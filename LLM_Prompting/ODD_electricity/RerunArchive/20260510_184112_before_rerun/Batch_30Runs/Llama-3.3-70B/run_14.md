### Action Situation 6: Social Learning and Capacitor Adoption
#### Tension: Imitation vs. Independent Decision
#### Sequential Representation:
1. Farmer 1 adopts capacitor or not
2. Farmer 2 observes outcome and decides to adopt or not

- If Farmer 1 adopts and fails: Farmer 2 doesn't adopt (1, 3)
- If Farmer 1 doesn't adopt: Farmer 2 doesn't adopt (2, 2)
#### Justification: Farmers observe and imitate successful neighbors, but failed adoption can discourage later uptake, reflecting path-dependent diffusion.

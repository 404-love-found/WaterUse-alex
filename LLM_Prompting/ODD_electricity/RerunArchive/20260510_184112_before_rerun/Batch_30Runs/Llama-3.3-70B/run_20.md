#### Tension: Farmers must decide whether to invest in capacitors to improve voltage stability, with mutual investment yielding shared improvement.

### Action Situation 6: Social Learning and Capacitor Adoption
#### Title: Sequential Social Learning
#### Tension: Farmers observe and imitate successful peers, with diffusion dependent on visible adoption and social networks.
#### Sequential Representation:

#### Justification: This action situation captures the sequential process of social learning in capacitor adoption, where farmers imitate successful peers and diffusion occurs through social networks.

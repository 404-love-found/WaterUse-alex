### Action Situation 5: Authorization and Enforcement
#### Title: Formal Authorization and Staff Enforcement
#### Tension: Formal authorization increases legitimacy, but staff may withhold effort to avoid maintenance burden.
#### Sequential Representation:
1. Farmer requests formal access.
2. Staff decides to invest in capacity or maintenance.
   - If staff invest, reliability improves, and penalties are avoided.
   - If staff withhold, farmer bears costs without receiving expected reliability improvements.
#### Justification: This action situation reflects the sequential interaction between farmers and staff, where formal authorization and staff enforcement influence reliability and penalties.

### Action Situation 6: Social Learning and Capacitor Adoption
#### Title: Diffusion of Capacitor Adoption
#### Tension: Farmers observe visible adoption by neighbors and may imitate successful peers.
#### Sequential Representation:
1. Farmer observes neighbor's capacitor adoption and outcome.
2. Farmer decides to adopt or not, based on observed outcome.
   - If farmer adopts, diffusion occurs.
   - If farmer! does not adopt, diffusion is slowed.
#### Justification: This action situation represents the social learning process, where farmers observe and imitate successful neighbors, influencing the diffusion of capacitor adoption.

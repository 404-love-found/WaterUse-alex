### Action Situation 1: Capacitor Adoption Assurance
#### Tension: 
Coordination problem among farmers to invest in voltage-stabilizing equipment.
#### Matrix:
|  | Farmer 2 Adopts | Farmer 2 Doesn't Adopt |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Farmer 1 Adopts** | 3, 3 | 1, 2 |
| **Farmer 1 Doesn't Adopt** | 2, 1 | 2, 2 |
#### Justification: 
Farmers face a dilemma where mutual adoption of capacitors yields a better outcome (3, 3) than unilateral adoption (1, 2) or no adoption (2, 2), reflecting the need for coordination.

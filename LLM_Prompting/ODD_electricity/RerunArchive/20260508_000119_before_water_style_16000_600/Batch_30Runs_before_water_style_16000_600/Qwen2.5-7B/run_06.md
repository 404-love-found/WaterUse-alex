### Action Situation 1: Capacitor Adoption Assurance Game (AS1)

#### Title: Capacitor Adoption Assurance Game (AS1)
#### Tension: Mutual Investment vs. Unilateral Investment
#### Matrix:
|               | Farmer 2: Invest | Farmer 2: Not Invest |
|---------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| **Farmer 1: Invest** | (3, 3)          | (2, 1)              |
| **Farmer 1: Not Invest** | (1, 2)          | (2, 2)              |

#### Justification:
- **Farmer 1 and Farmer 2** share a transformer. Mutual investment in capacitors improves voltage stability and pump efficiency, benefiting both. Unilateral investment does not yield private benefits, leading to a coordination problem.
- **(3, 3)**: Both farmers invest, leading to shared benefits.
- **(2, 1)** and **(1, 2)**: One farmer invests while the other does not, yielding no private benefit for the investor.
- **(2, 2)**: Neither farmer invests, resulting in a low but non-zero baseline.

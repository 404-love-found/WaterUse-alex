#### Title: Coordination for Voltage-Stabilising Equipment
#### Tension: Farmers must decide whether to invest in capacitors, with mutual investment yielding shared improvement.
#### Matrix:
|  | Farmer 2 Invests | Farmer 2 Does Not Invest |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Farmer 1 Invests** | 3, 3 | 1, 2 |

#### Justification: This action situation reflects the strategic tension between farmers deciding to invest in capacitors. Mutual investment yields a higher payoff (3, 3), while unilateral investment yields a lower payoff (1, 2), and no investment yields a baseline payoff (2, 2).

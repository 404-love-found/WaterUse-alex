### Action Situation 1: Capacitor Adoption Assurance Game
#### Title: Farmer-Farmer Coordination
#### Tension: Mutual cooperation for voltage-stabilising equipment investment
#### Matrix:
|  | Farmer 2 Invests | Farmer 2 Doesn't Invest |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Farmer 1 Invests** | 3, 3 | 1, 2 |
| **Farmer 1 Doesn't Invest** | 2, 1 | 2, 2 |
#### Justification: This game reflects the strategic tension between two neighbouring farmers deciding whether to invest in voltage-stabilising equipment. Mutual investment yields shared improvement (3, 3), while unilateral investment yields no added private benefit (1, 2 or 2, 1), creating a coordination problem.

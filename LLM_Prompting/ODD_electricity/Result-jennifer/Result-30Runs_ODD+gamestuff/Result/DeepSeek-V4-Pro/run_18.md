# Run 18 — deepseek-ai/DeepSeek-V4-Pro

**Action Situation 1: DSM Coordination Game**  
*Location*: Transformer service area – among farmers sharing the same electricity transformer.  
*Players*: Two representative farmers from the same transformer group.  
*Roles*: Electricity consumers deciding whether to invest in demand‑side management (capacitor) equipment.  
*Actions*: Each farmer chooses **Invest (I)** or **Not invest (N)**.  
*Control Rules*: If both invest, voltage stability improves, reducing pump burnouts and energy waste for the whole group. If only one invests, the adopter bears the full cost but sees no reliability gain because aggregate load still causes voltage drops. If neither invests, the status quo of poor voltage quality persists.  
*Information*: Farmers know past adoption rates and experienced voltage quality but cannot perfectly predict the other’s simultaneous choice.

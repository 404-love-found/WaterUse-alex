### Action Situation 1: Capacitor Adoption Assurance Game
#### Title: Farmer-Farmer Coordination
#### Tension: Mutual cooperation for voltage stabilisation
#### Matrix/Sequential Representation:
|  | Farmer 2 Invests | Farmer 2 Does Not Invest |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Farmer 1 Invests** | 3, 3 | 1, 2 |
| **Farmer 1 Does Not Invest** | 2, 1 | 2, 2 |
#### Justification: This action situation reflects the strategic tension between two neighbouring farmers deciding whether to invest in voltage-stabilising equipment (capacitors). Mutual investment yields shared improvement, while unilateral investment yields no added private benefit.

### Action Situation 2: Sequential Social Learning in Capacitor Adoption
#### Title: Farmer Social Learning
#### Tension: Imitation of successful peers
#### Matrix/Sequential Representation:
- Farmer 1 adopts capacitor
  - Farmer 2 observes success: adopts capacitor
  - Farmer 2 observes failure: does not adopt capacitor
- Farmer 1 does not adopt capacitor
  - Farmer 2 does not adopt capacitor
#### Justification: This sequential action situation represents how farmers observe their peers' outcomes and imitate successful capacitor adoption, leading to diffusion.

### Action Situation 2: Sequential Social-Learning Process in Capacitor Adoption
#### Title: Observational Learning and Imitation
#### Tension: Adoption Decision Based on Observed Outcomes
#### Matrix/Sequential Representation:
- Farmer 1 adopts capacitor
  - Farmer 2 observes outcome
    - If outcome is successful, Farmer 2 adopts
    - If outcome is unsuccessful, Farmer 2 does not adopt
- Farmer 1 does not adopt capacitor
  - Farmer 2 does not adopt
#### Justification: AS2 is a sequential process where farmers observe and imitate successful peers, leading to the diffusion of capacitor adoption based on observed outcomes.

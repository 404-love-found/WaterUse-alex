### Action Situation 1: Capacitor-Adoption Assurance Game
#### Title: Farmer-Farmer Coordination for Voltage Stabilization
#### Tension: Mutual cooperation for shared improvement vs. unilateral investment with no added private benefit
#### Matrix/Sequential Representation:
|  | Farmer 2 Adopts | Farmer 2 Does Not Adopt |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Farmer 1 Adopts** | 3, 3 | 1, 2 |

### Action Situation 6: Sequential Social-Learning Process in Capacitor Adoption
#### Title: Farmer-Farmer Social Learning for Capacitor Adoption
#### Tension: Imitation of successful peers vs. risk of unsuccessful investment
#### Sequential Representation:
1. Farmer 1 adopts capacitor or not.
2. Farmer 2 observes Farmer 1's outcome and decides to adopt or not.
3. Payoffs are determined by the adoption decisions and outcomes.
#### Justification: This action situation represents the sequential social-learning process in capacitor adoption, where each farmer observes a peer's outcome and imitates only if that outcome ranks higher.

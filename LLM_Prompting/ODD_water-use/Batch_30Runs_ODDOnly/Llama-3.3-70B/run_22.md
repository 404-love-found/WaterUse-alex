#### Tension: Water Scarcity
#### Matrix:

| --- | --- | --- |
| **Downstream Farmer (Cooperate)** | 5, 5 | 2, 8 |
| **Downstream Farmer (Defect)** | 8, 2 | 3, 3 |

#### Justification: In the decentralized case, each farmer decides on the number of fields to irrigate based on their prediction of water flows and available budget. The upstream farmer's decision affects the downstream farmer's water availability. If both cooperate (irrigate fewer fields), they both get a moderate payoff (5). If the upstream farmer defects (irrigates more fields), they get a higher payoff (8), but the downstream farmer gets a lower payoff (2). If both defect, they both get a low payoff (3) due to water scarcity.

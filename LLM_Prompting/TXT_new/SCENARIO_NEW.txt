Action Situations & Strategic Triggers

Based on the ODD+D and Game Logic, the following 4 distinct Action Situations must be solved by the agents:

---
SCENARIO 1: "The Forecast Bet" (Early Season)
- Type: Trust Game / Signal Game
- Trigger: National Authority (NA) releases a water forecast.
- Players: NA (Sender) vs. Farmers (Receivers).
- Strategic Tension:
  - NA wants farmers to plant efficiently but fears panic. NA might overestimate water to encourage planting.
  - Farmers must decide: "Do I trust the forecast and plant 10 fields? Or do I distrust it and play safe with 4 fields?"
- Payoff Risk: Trusting a wrong forecast leads to -100 (Crop Failure). Distrusting a good forecast leads to Opportunity Cost.

---
SCENARIO 2: "The Upstream Temptation" (Mid Season - Irrigation)
- Type: Asymmetric Commons Dilemma
- Trigger: Water arrives at Upstream gates.
- Players: Upstream Farmer (UF) vs. Downstream Farmer (DF).
- Strategic Tension:
  - UF moves First. UF can take ALL the water (High Extraction).
  - IF UF takes High: DF gets Zero water (Sucker's Payoff).
  - IF UF takes Low: DF gets water, but UF sacrifices potential profit.
- Key Insight: This is NOT a standard Prisoner's Dilemma because payoffs are not symmetric. UF dominates.

---
SCENARIO 3: "The Ecological Cliff" (Late Season - Environmental)
- Type: Stag Hunt / Threshold Public Goods Game
- Trigger: Determining water residual for the Lake.
- Players: All Farmers collectively.
- Strategic Tension:
  - To save the fish (High Future Payoff), TOTAL extraction must be low.
  - If even one agent defects and extracts High, the threshold is missed, and the fish die (Disaster for all).
  - Equilibrium:
    1. Cooperation: Everyone extracts Low -> Fish Survive -> High Long-term Utility.
    2. Defection: Everyone extracts High -> Fish Die -> Ecosystem Collapse.

---
SCENARIO 4: "The Downstream Desperation" (Reactive Phase)
- Type: Chicken Game / Survival Game
- Trigger: UF has already extracted High.
- Players: DF vs. Nature.
- Strategic Tension:
  - UF left very little water.
  - DF Choice:
    1. Extract the little remaining water for Crops (Kill the fish immediately).
    2. Sacrifice Crops to save the Fish (Hope for future fish income).
  - This tests the agent's time horizon (Short-term hunger vs. Long-term sustainability).

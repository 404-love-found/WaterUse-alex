### Action Situation 1: Queue Access and Fairness
#### Title: Resident vs Non-Resident Queue Priority
#### Tension: Strategic tension between residents and non-residents over queue priority and access to limited charging points.
#### Matrix:
|  | Resident Waits | Resident Jumps Queue |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Non-Resident Waits** | R:3, NR:3 | R:5, NR:1 |
| **Non-Resident Jumps Queue** | R:1, NR:5 | R:2, NR:2 |
#### Justification: Residents may feel entitled to priority access due to their discount and housing relationship, while non-residents may believe they deserve equal treatment after paying the regular rate. This tension can lead to conflicts over queue jumping and fairness.

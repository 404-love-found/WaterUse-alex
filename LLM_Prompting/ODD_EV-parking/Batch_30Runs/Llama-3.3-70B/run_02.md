### Action Situation 1: Queue Access and Fairness
#### Title: Queue Jumping Dilemma
#### Tension: Resident vs. Non-Resident User
#### Matrix:

|  | Resident Waits | Resident Jumps Queue |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Non-Resident Waits** | R: 3, NR: 3 | R: 5, NR: 1 |
| **Non-Resident Jumps Queue** | R: 1, NR: 5 | R: 2, NR: 2 |

#### Justification: This action situation reflects the strategic tension between a resident and a non-resident user over queue access. If both wait, they receive a moderate payoff (3). If one jumps the queue, they receive a higher payoff (5), while the other receives a lower payoff (1). If both jump, they both receive a low payoff (2) due to conflict and potential staff intervention.

#### Justification: This action situation reflects the strategic tension between a user and staff over charger occupation and move-out behavior.

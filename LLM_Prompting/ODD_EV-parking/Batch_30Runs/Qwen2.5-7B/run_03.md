#### Title
Bypassing the Queue vs. Adhering to Queue Order

#### Tension
Residents and non-residents must decide whether to bypass the queue or adhere to the posted order.

| **Non-Resident Adheres to Queue** | (2, 0) | (1, 1) |

- **Non-Resident Adheres to Queue**: Both residents and non-residents have a medium wait, but the non-resident's fairness is maintained.

This matrix captures the trade-off between immediate access and fairness.

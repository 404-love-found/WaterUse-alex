### Title: Strategic Tensions in Shared EV Charging

### Tension 1: Queue Bypass vs. Queue Compliance

**Matrix:**

|                   | Resident Bypasses Queue | Resident Complies Queue |
|-------------------|------------------------|------------------------|
| **Non-resident Bypasses Queue** | (1, 1)                  | (1, 2)                 |
| **Non-resident Complies Queue** | (2, 1)                  | (2, 2)                 |

**Justification:**
Residents and non-residents face a dilemma when queue order is at stake. Residents may see bypassing as unfair but also believe they have stronger claims to the charger. Non-residents may comply but resent the perceived unfairness. Both parties benefit if the queue is bypassed, but strict compliance by both reduces conflict and improves fairness.

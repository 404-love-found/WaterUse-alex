### Title: Strategic Tensions in Shared EV Charging at Apartment Garages

### 1. Resident vs. Non-Resident Charging Fairness

#### Tension: Fairness in Queue Access

**Matrix:**

|                  | Resident Follows Queue | Resident Bypasses Queue |
|------------------|-----------------------|------------------------|
| **Non-Resident Follows Queue** | (1, 1)                 | (0, 2)                 |
| **Non-Resident Bypasses Queue** | (2, 0)                 | (0, 0)                 |

**Justification:** 
Residents benefit directly from bypassing the queue, while non-residents face longer wait times. Non-residents also face a higher risk of being penalized for bypassing the queue, which can lead to higher complaints and reduced trust in the system.

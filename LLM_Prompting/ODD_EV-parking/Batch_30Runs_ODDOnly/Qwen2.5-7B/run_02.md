### Title: Strategic Tensions in Shared EV Charging Governance

#### 1. Resident vs. Non-Resident Charging Access

**Tension:**
Residents and non-residents face a strategic tension over EV charging access in an apartment parking facility. Residents benefit from a discounted per-kWh rate, which can lead to increased demand and potentially longer wait times for non-residents.

**Matrix:**

|                   | Non-Residents Wait | Non-Residents Leave |
|-------------------|--------------------|---------------------|
| **Residents Wait** | Residents: -1, Non-Residents: -1 | Residents: 0, Non-Residents: -2 |
| **Residents Leave**| Residents: -2, Non-Residents: 0 | Residents: 0, Non-Residents: 0 |

**Justification:**
- **Residents Wait:** If residents wait, they may secure a charger but delay their own charging, causing mutual dissatisfaction.
- **Residents Leave:** If residents leave, they may avoid delays but allow non-residents to use the charger, potentially reducing their own charging time.
- **Non-Residents Wait:** If non-residents wait, they may face longer wait times and dissatisfaction.
- **Non-Residents Leave:** If non-residents leave, they avoid delays but reduce their charging time.

#### 2. Queue Bypass vs. Queue Compliance

**Tension:**
Residents and non-residents face a strategic tension over whether to bypass the queue for EV charging. Bypassing can lead to shorter wait times for the individual but may cause frustration for others in the queue.

**Matrix:**

|                   | Queue Bypass | Queue Compliance |
|-------------------|--------------|------------------|
| **Queue Bypass**   | Residents: -1, Non-Residents: -1 | Residents: 1, Non-Residents: -2 |
| **Queue Compliance** | Residents: -2, Non-Residents: 1 | Residents: 0, Non-Residents: 0 |

**Justification:**
- **Queue Bypass:** If both residents and non-residents bypass the queue, both may face shorter wait times but mutual dissatisfaction.
- **Queue Compliance:** If both comply with the queue, both may face longer wait times but mutual cooperation.
- **Residents Bypass:** If residents bypass and non-residents comply, residents benefit but non-residents suffer.
- **Non-Residents Bypass:** If non-residents bypass and residents comply, non-residents benefit but residents suffer.

#### 3. Charging Bay Occupation vs. Prompt Move-Out

**Tension:**
Residents and non-residents face a strategic tension over whether to leave the charging bay promptly after useful charging or remain and block access for others.

**Matrix:**

|                   | Prompt Move-Out | Remain in Bay |
|-------------------|-----------------|---------------|
| **Prompt Move-Out** | Residents: 1, Non-Residents: 1 | Residents: -1, Non-Residents: 2 |
| **Remain in Bay**   | Residents: 2, Non-Residents: -1 | Residents: -2, Non-Residents: -2 |

**Justification:**
- **Prompt Move-Out:** If both leave promptly, both benefit from quick access.
- **Remain in Bay:** If both remain, access is blocked, causing mutual dissatisfaction.
- **Residents Remain:** If residents remain and non-residents leave, residents suffer but non-residents benefit.
- **Non-Residents Remain:** If non-residents remain and residents leave, non-residents suffer but residents benefit.

#### 4. Staff Enforcement vs. Informal Requests

**Tension:**
Parking-lot management staff and residents face a strategic tension over whether to enforce queue rules or grant informal exceptions for residents.

**Matrix:**

|                   | Enforce Queue Rules | Grant Informal Requests |
|-------------------|---------------------|-------------------------|
| **Enforce Queue Rules** | Residents: -1, Staff: -1 | Residents: 1, Staff: -2 |
| **Grant Informal Requests** | Residents: -2, Staff: 1 | Residents: 0, Staff: 0 |

**Justification:**
- **Enforce Queue Rules:** If both enforce, queue fairness is maintained but residents may suffer.
- **Grant Informal Requests:** If both grant, residents benefit but staff face complaints.
- **Staff Enforce:** If staff enforce and residents request, staff suffer but residents benefit.
- **Residents Request:** If residents request and staff enforce, residents suffer but staff benefit.

#### 5. Resident Discount Eligibility vs. Fairness

**Tension:**
Residents and non-residents face a strategic tension over whether the resident discount is perceived as fair and justified.

**Matrix:**

|                   | Fair Discount | Unfair Discount |
|-------------------|---------------|-----------------|
| **Fair Discount**  | Residents: 1, Non-Residents: -1 | Residents: -1, Non-Residents: 1 |
| **Unfair Discount** | Residents: -1, Non-Residents: 1 | Residents: 1, Non-Residents: -1 |

**Justification:**
- **Fair Discount:** If both perceive the discount as fair, residents benefit and non-residents suffer.
- **Unfair Discount:** If both perceive the discount as unfair, non-residents benefit and residents suffer.
- **Residents Perceive Fair:** If residents perceive fair and non-residents perceive unfair, residents benefit and non-residents suffer.
- **Non-Residents Perceive Fair:** If non-residents perceive fair and residents perceive unfair, non-residents benefit and residents suffer.

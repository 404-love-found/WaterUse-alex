### Title: Strategic Tensions in Shared EV Charging at Apartment Parking Facilities

#### 1. Resident vs. Non-Resident Queue Bypassing

**Tension:** Residents vs. Non-Residents
**Matrix:**
```
          Non-Residents
          Bypass Queue    Follow Queue
Residents Bypass Queue  (1, 1)        (2, 0)
Follow Queue           (0, 2)        (1, 1)
```
**Justification:** Residents and non-residents face a strategic dilemma over whether to bypass the queue. If both follow the queue, the system is fair but slower. If one bypasses, the non-bypassing party gains faster access but at the expense of fairness. The payoffs reflect the perceived fairness and speed benefits.

### Action Situation Analysis

1. **Queue Jumping**
	* Tension: User decides whether to follow queue rules or jump ahead, while staff decides whether to enforce queue order.
	* Matrix:
		|  | Enforce | Don't Enforce |
		| --- | --- | --- |
		| Follow Queue | 3, 3 | 1, 5 |
		| Jump Queue | 5, 1 | 2, 2 |
	* Justification: This action situation reflects the strategic tension between users who want to minimize their wait time and staff who aim to maintain queue fairness.

2. **Charger Occupation**
	* Tension: User decides whether to occupy a charger for an extended period, while staff decides whether to intervene and ask the user to move.
	* Matrix:
		|  | Intervene | Don't Intervene |
		| --- | --- | --- |
		| Occupy Charger | 1, 5 | 5, 1 |
		| Release Charger | 3, 3 | 2, 2 |
	* Justification: This action situation represents the conflict between users who want to maximize their charging time and staff who need to ensure charger availability for other users.

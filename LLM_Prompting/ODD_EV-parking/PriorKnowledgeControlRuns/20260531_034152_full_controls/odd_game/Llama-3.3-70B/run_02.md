**Tension:** Residents and non-resident users competing for limited charging points, with residents receiving a discounted per-kWh rate, may lead to conflicts over queue priority and access.
**Matrix:**
| User Type / Staff | Follow Queue | Bypass Queue |
|---|---|---|
| Follow Queue | (predictable access, low complaints) | (delayed access, high complaints) |
| Bypass Queue | (immediate access, potential conflict) | (immediate access, potential conflict) |
**Justification:** This is an action situation because users must decide whether to follow the queue or bypass it, and staff must decide how to enforce queue rules, leading to potential conflicts and trade-offs between access, complaints, and fairness.

### Action Situation 1: Queue Access
**Tension:** Residents and non-resident users compete for limited charger access, with residents receiving a discounted per-kWh rate, potentially leading to conflicts over queue priority.
**Matrix:**
| Player (Resident/Non-Resident) / Action | Join Queue | Bypass Queue |
|---|---|---|
| Join Queue | (predictable wait, fair access) | (longer wait, potential conflict) |
| Bypass Queue | (shorter wait, potential conflict) | (unpredictable wait, unfair access) |
**Justification:** This is an action situation because residents and non-resident users have different incentives and preferences regarding queue access, leading to potential conflicts and trade-olds between fairness, wait time, and access to chargers.

### Action Situation 1: Upstream Farmer's Irrigation Decision
#### Title: Irrigation Dilemma
#### Tension: Over-irrigation vs. Conservation
#### Matrix:

|  | Downstream Farmer Cooperates (Conserves Water) | Downstream Farmer Defects (Over-irrigates) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Upstream Farmer Cooperates (Conserves Water)** | 8, 8 | 5, 9 |
| **Upstream Farmer Defects (Over-irrigates)** | 9, 5 | 3, 3 |

#### Justification: In the decentralized case, an upstream farmer's decision to irrigate affects the downstream farmer's water availability. If both farmers cooperate and conserve water, they both receive a moderate payoff (8). However, if the upstream farmer defects and over-irrigates, they receive a higher payoff (9), while the downstream farmer's payoff decreases (5). If both farmers defect, they both receive a low payoff (3) due to water scarcity and ecological degradation.

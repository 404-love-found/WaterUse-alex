### Decentralized Water Use Action Situations
#### 1. Upstream Farmer's Irrigation Decision
**Title:** Upstream Irrigation Dilemma
**Tension:** Whether to irrigate the maximum number of fields (10) or conserve water for downstream farmers.
**Matrix:**

|  | Downstream Conserves | Downstream Irrigates |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Upstream Conserves** | 5, 5 | 3, 7 |
| **Upstream Irrigates** | 7, 3 | 2, 2 |

**Justification:** The upstream farmer has to decide whether to conserve water or irrigate the maximum number of fields. If the downstream farmer also irrigates, the upstream farmer's payoff decreases due to reduced water availability downstream. The matrix reflects the spatial asymmetry, where the upstream farmer's actions affect the downstream farmer's payoff more significantly.

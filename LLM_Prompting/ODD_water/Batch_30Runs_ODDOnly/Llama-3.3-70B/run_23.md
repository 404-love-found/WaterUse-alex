### Action Situation 1: Upstream-Downstream Water Extraction
#### Tension: Overextraction of Water Resources
#### Matrix:

|  | Upstream Cooperates | Upstream Defects |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Downstream Cooperates** | 5, 5 | 2, 8 |
| **Downstream Defects** | 8, 2 | 3, 3 |

#### Justification: 
In the decentralized case, each farmer decides on the number of fields to irrigate based on their prediction of water flows and available budget. The upstream farmer has an advantage in terms of accessing water, while the downstream farmer is at a disadvantage. If both farmers cooperate and extract water sustainably, they both get a moderate payoff (5). If the upstream farmer defects and extracts more water, they get a higher payoff (8), while the downstream farmer gets a lower payoff (2). If both farmers defect, they both get a low payoff (3) due to overextraction and water scarcity.

1. odd vs ODD_NEW (Overview & Concepts)

Old: A detailed technical manual with complex simulation parameters (e.g., 200-year spans, 12 fish age classes).

New: A strategic framework designed for LLMs. It removes heavy details and focuses on agent psychology ("Asymmetry Perception") and the core conflict between Upstream and Downstream farmers.

2. game_stuff vs GAME_STUFF_NEW (Math & Logic)

Old: Uses complex calculus, algebraic formulas, and matrix models to calculate yields and fish populations.

New: Translates complex math into simple, discrete rules (e.g., "max 10 fields") and introduces a hard "ecological tipping point" (e.g., "lake needs 5 units of water"), making it highly digestible for an AI.

3. scenarios vs SCENARIO_NEW (Action Situations)

Old: Acts like an answer key, providing pre-calculated payoff matrices and solved Nash equilibria.

New: Removes all pre-calculated numbers. Instead, it provides narrative "Strategic Triggers" (e.g., "The Ecological Cliff") to force the LLM to reason and generate its own payoff matrices from scratch.
